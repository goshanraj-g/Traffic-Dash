How to Play

Use the Arrow keys or A/D to move left/right.

Avoid colliding with NPC vehicles. Colliding 10 times ends the game.

Your score increases over time, and rounds get harder!

At 100 points, you'll switch to a bike and NPCs speed up (Medium round).
At 200 points, it goes to Hard (even faster NPCs, longer spawn delays, and more cars on screen).

Game Over!
